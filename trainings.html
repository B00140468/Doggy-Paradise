TRAININGS

Training your dog is crucial for their physical and mental wellbeing, as well as for strengthening the bond between you and your furry friend.

OUR TRAININGS

Basic Obedience Training

This training will teach your dog basic commands such as sit, stay, come, and heel, which makes it easier for you and us to manage the dogs and for the dogs to adapt to new environments

Socialisation Training

This training helps dogs to behave appropriately around others and reduces the risk of aggressive behaviour.

Crate Training

This training teaches dogs to view their crate as a comfortable and positive place, which can help reduce anxiety and stress during their stay.
